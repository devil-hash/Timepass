Dheekshitha Venkadeshan (born 26 June 1998), known professionally as Dhee, is an Australian singer known for her work as a playback singer in Indian cinema.Born to a Sri Lankan father and mother, she was raised in Sydney and has sung songs mostly in her stepfather Santhosh Narayanan's albums. She has won a Filmfare Awards for Best Singer,and is known for her distinctive alto voice
